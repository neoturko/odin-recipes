Turkish Lentil Soup

A hearty and comforting Turkish lentil soup, made with red lentils, vegetables, and warm spices. This simple yet delicious dish is a staple in Turkish cuisine and perfect for any season.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 5 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Steps:

  1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion, carrot, and potato; sauté for about 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cumin; cook for another minute to release the aroma.
  4. Add the red lentils and water (or broth). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes, until the lentils and vegetables are tender.
  5. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth, or leave it chunky if you prefer.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side for squeezing.
