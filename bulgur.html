Turkish Bulgur Pilaf

A traditional and easy side dish made with bulgur wheat, tomatoes, and onions. A healthy and filling addition to any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup bulgur wheat (medium grain)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil or butter in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and sauté until softened and translucent.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  4. Add bulgur and mix well to coat with the tomato-onion mixture.
  5. Pour in water or broth, season with salt and pepper, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes until the bulgur absorbs the liquid.
  7. Fluff with a fork and serve warm.
