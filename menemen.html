Menemen (Turkish Scrambled Eggs with Vegetables)

A quick and flavorful Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and spices. Perfect for a simple, hearty meal.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil/li>
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced green peppers and sauté for 3–4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add diced tomatoes and cook for another 5–6 minutes until they release their juices.
  4. Stir in red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and gently stir until just set.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread.
